Different windows present in Numpy for leakage¶

  1. blackman
  2. bartlett
  3. hamming
  4. hanning

1.Blackman¶

for window_func in [np.bartlett, np.blackman, np.hamming, np.hanning]:
    wave7 = signal.make_wave(duration)
    wave7.ys *= window_func(len(wave.ys))

    spectrum = wave7.make_spectrum()
    spectrum.plot(high=880, label=window_func.__name__)
